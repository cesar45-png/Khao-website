Khao Tom Mud

Khao tom mud is a traditional Thai and Laotian dessert made of sweet rice(glutinous rice) and sweet banana or other filling, flavored with coconut milk, wrapped in banana leaves, and then steamed

Ingredients

  • 8 Cups sweet rice or Thai glutinous rice
  • 2 (400ml)cans coconut milk
  • 2 1/2 cups sugar (use less for a less sweet taste)
  • [Filler] Sliced taro, banana, or sweet potatoes
  • Banana Leaves

Instructions

  1. Prepare the ingredients:
  • rinse the sweet rice thoroughly and
    soak it in water for at least 2 hours

  • Rinse banana leaves well and cut
    them into rectangular sheets. Briefly
    steam or blanch them to make them pliable.
  1. Cook The Rice
  • In a large pot combine the drained glutinous rice,
    coconut milk, and sugar.
  • Cook on low heat, stirring occasionally, until the rice
    absorbs most of the liquid but is only partially cooked.
    This should take around 20-30 minutes.
  1. Assembling
  • Lay a banana leaf flat, shiny side up, and place a mound
    of the partially cooked rice mixture in the center.
  • Add a piece of filler on top of the rice.
  • Fold the banana leaf around the rice to encase it completely
    in a rectangular package. You can tie the package with string
    or a strip of a banana leaf.
  1. Steam
  • Steam the packages over medium heat for
    about 40-50 minutes until the rice is fully cooked
    and the smell is fragrant.
  1. Serve
  • Let cool slightly and serve warm or at room temperature.
    Enjoy this delicious, traditional Thai dessert!!!
